Product Details - Legume Silage

Process of Making Legume Silage

Legume silage is made from crops like clover, alfalfa, and peas. These legumes are harvested, fermented, and stored to enhance their nutritional value for livestock feed.

The legumes are cut, fermented in silos or wrapped bales, ensuring they are preserved and packed with nutrients for livestock consumption.

Ingredients in Legume Silage

  • Clover, alfalfa, peas

  • High protein content

  • Rich in minerals

  • Excellent fiber source

Nutrients in Legume Silage

  • High in protein

  • Rich in fiber

  • Essential vitamins

  • Minerals like calcium and magnesium

Benefits of Legume Silage for Livestock

  • Boosts milk production

  • Enhances weight gain

  • Improves feed conversion

  • Supports immune health
