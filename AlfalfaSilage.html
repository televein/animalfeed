Product Details - Alfalfa Silage

Process of Making Alfalfa Silage

Alfalfa silage is made from the fermentation of freshly cut alfalfa, a nutrient-dense legume.

The crop is chopped, compacted, and stored under anaerobic conditions to undergo natural fermentation.

Ingredients in Alfalfa Silage

  • Fresh-cut alfalfa

  • High protein content

  • Fiber for digestive health

  • Essential minerals and vitamins

Nutrients in Alfalfa Silage

  • Rich in protein

  • Essential vitamins like A, D, and E

  • High in calcium and potassium

  • Supports immune function

Benefits of Alfalfa Silage for Cattle

  • Boosts milk production in dairy cattle

  • Supports muscle growth and repair

  • Improves feed efficiency

  • Enhances overall health
